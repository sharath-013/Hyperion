Welcome to the Simulation.

Hyperion is a fast-paced, high-stakes 5v5 battle arena. You take control of a "Shard"—a powerful avatar—and work with your team to dismantle the enemy's defenses and destroy their Stronghold, a central core of power.

  • Choose Your Guardian: Master unique abilities from a vast roster.
  • Battle & Evolve: Gain power, buy items, and control the map.
  • Destroy the Core: Break the enemy Stronghold to claim victory.
